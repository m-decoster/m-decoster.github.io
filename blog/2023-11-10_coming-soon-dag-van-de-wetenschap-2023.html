Coming soon: Dag Van De Wetenschap 2023

10 November 2023

My colleagues and I will be presenting my work on video-based sign language dictionary querying as a demo at the Dag Van De Wetenschap in Ghent on November 26th, 2023.

During this demo, interested people will be able to try out the search functionality prototype for the Flemish sign language dictionary. This prototype is one of the outcomes of the past several years of my PhD research into sign language recognition and I am very excited to share this outcome with the general public!

The source code of this demo will soon be available on GitHub.
