New article: 'Machine translation from signed to spoken languages: state of the art and challenges'

06 April 2023

Machine translation for sign languages is a hot topic. Last week, my most recent article was published in Springer's Universal Access in the Information Society.

The article describes how machine translation from signed to spoken languages is performed, what is required, and what are open challenges in the domain (spoiler: there are plenty). Interestingly especially for computer and data scientists, it also touches on some sign language linguistics in section 2.

The article is published in open access and you can access it here.
