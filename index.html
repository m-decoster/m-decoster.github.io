PhD, Robotics and AI Researcher

Hi! I'm Mathieu. I have a PhD in computer science engineering. My interests lie in data science and artificial intelligence, and I am also fascinated by graphics programming. Currently, I am working on intelligent robotic manipulation at IDLab-AIRO.

I obtained my PhD from Ghent University in 2024. My research focused on sign language recognition and translation and it has led to several high impact publications and a follow-up valorisation project.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, photography, piano and cooking.
