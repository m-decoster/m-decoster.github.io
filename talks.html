Talks

I am open to giving talks and tutorials on subjects related to AI, sign language processing, and software engineering and I'm registered as an expert on VAIA, the Flemish AI academy.

Over the past few years, I've developed my personal presentation style, which is also reflected in the design of this website. It's built for clarity and impact: a high signal-to-noise ratio, clean, and to the point. I strip away distractions to focus on the main message, without sacrificing engagement. The result is a presentation that respects your time, communicates clearly, and stays sharp and interesting from start to finish.
